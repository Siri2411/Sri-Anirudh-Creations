What We Do

Pre Press

Our pre-press facility is equipped with high end equipment, Software and Hardware to meet your needs. The pre-press is equipped with Kodak Trendsetter equipment and software. The highly trained pre-press team ensures that your job is processed to get the best results and is committed to enhance value to your print product. Any job order coming to the pre-press department is first checked for its quality and resolution and any errors are fixed at the stage itself. After processing, proofs are taken on a caliberated ink jet proofer and sent to the customers for approval. These inkjet proofs provide as close a match as possible with the printed press sheet. After receiving the approval the job is ready to print. Digital worklow of fully trapped and imposed files with color, bars,cut and fold marks which ensures perfect registeration and sharp printing on the press with minimum wastage. There is manual stripping or imposing involved here; since these are done by Kodak preps imposing software and the pages on our new Kodak computer to plate(CTP) machine with quantum engine ensures perfect registeration.
